 Our college is collaborating with Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) in the Academic Interface Program (AIP) like SELENIUM –Testing Tool an initiative of TCS in offering employment oriented education to the students which will strengthen the Industry-Institute relationship by grooming the students to the competitive world. The Spoken Tutorial project is being developed by IIT Bombay for MHRD, Government of India.

One of the most popular undergraduate courses these days, BCA degree is a stepping stone towards lucrative and advanced career options in the field of Information Technology. The ever increasing requirement for programming experts and software developers due to the rapid growth of IT industry in India and abroad has created a lot of opportunities for BCA graduates.

The Spoken Tutorial project is being developed by IIT Bombay for MHRD, Government of India. It is a three year undergraduate degree course for students from all backgrounds (Arts, Science and Commerce). This course introduces students to the world of computers, computer languages and computer applications. A BCA degree is considered almost equivalent to a Bachelor of Technology (B. Tech) or Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Computer Science or Information Technology (IT).

The basic course curriculum of BCA comprises database management systems (DBMS), data structure, operating systems (OS), networking, software engineering, computer architecture, web technology and core programming languages like C, HTML, Java, Web Application Development, etc.
